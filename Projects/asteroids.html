Development - Christmas 2019

This project marked my introduction to the realm of computer science, igniting my passion for the field. My journey began by tinkering with graphics using JavaScript and the p5 library, creating intricate art through recursive algorithms like fractals. Seeking to delve further, I decided to develop a straightforward game that leveraged the p5 library for visuals, allowing me to craft everything else from scratch.

In this pursuit, I crafted a miniature matrix library to facilitate various calculations, encompassing collision detection and physics. The incorporation of noise enabled the creation of visually engaging asteroid shapes, albeit posing challenges in the implementation of collision detection. To enhance the gaming experience, I introduced a range of custom effects. For instance, animations were introduced for the ship's fiery thrust, particle effects emerged from asteroid collisions and splits, and pieces of the ship scattered upon impact.

This project, which originated as a simple game, led me down a path of exploration and discovery within computer science. It became a pivotal starting point that ignited my passion and set me on a trajectory of continuous learning and creativity.

Gameplay

The project features a rocket that boasts maneuverability, enabling turns and thrust with a visually appealing fire triangle effect. The rocket can also shoot bullets aimed at asteroids.

When these bullets collide with asteroids, the latter splits into smaller fragments, accompanied by an impactful shattering particle effect. The game structure incorporates limited lives, although the score persists until all lives are depleted.

The ship's motion is bounded by screen boundaries, creating a wrapping effect. Conversely, asteroids are confined within proximity to the screen. New asteroids with diverse velocities dynamically enter from the screen's edges. Their distinctive shapes are achieved by perturbing points of a circular polygon through random noise.

This project illustrates a skillful combination of gameplay mechanics and visual effects, delivering an engaging and interactive experience for players.
